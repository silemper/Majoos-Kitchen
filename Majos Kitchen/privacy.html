Important Notice for Catering Orders

Dear Valued Customers,


We appreciate your interest in our catering services! To ensure we meet your needs and provide the best quality experience, please be advised of the following:
  • Advance Notice: We kindly request a minimum of four weeks' notice for all catering orders. THis allows us to source the finest ingredients and prepare accordingly.

  • Order Confirmation: Once your order is received, we will send a confirmation outlining the details. Please review it carefully and notify us of any changes.

  • Payment Terms: A deposit will be required to secure your order, with the balance due prior to the event. Specific payment details will be provided upon confirmation.

  • Menu Selection: We offer a diverse range of menu options tailored to fit your event's theme and dietary preferences. Please let us know of any specific requirements.

  • Cancellation Policy: In the event of a cancellation, please refer to our policy for details on refunds and rescheduling.
Thank you for choosing our catering services. We look forward to making your event a memorable one!

Comfort Ayimah
Director
